Why This Exists

Tommy was a blue-grey Pitbull from Buxar, Bihar.
She looked at the world with complete trust.

She was lost because emergency veterinary care didn't reach Tier 3 India in time. She was the softest creature in the most misunderstood body.

Every portrait we create answers one question:
What if we had reached her in time?

A part of every order goes directly toward animal care and emergency veterinary service facilitation — so no family has to feel what we felt.
